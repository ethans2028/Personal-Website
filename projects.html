0. Predicting Student Dropouts

April - Pres

For my Machine Learning course, I am using Python to train, test, and validate a supervised machine learning model. My goal is to help predict student success rate. I hope my work is useful to institutions hoping to improve the identification of at risk students and assist them earlier.

The attributes present in my dataset include demographic, macro-economic, and academic information for each student. Each student is classified into 3 categories: dropout, enrolled, and graduate.
